Ameeera Cooperation


Ameera Coperation is a cooperative incorporated located in the Agung Semarang complex, Jalan Sisingamangaraja whose members consist of Teachers and Employees under the Foundation.

Ameera Coperation was founded on the thoughts of Mr. H.U.A.Komarudin,BA who at that time served as Vice Principal of Ameera International School Center and Mr. H. Moh. Hidayat Sumanta, who at that time served as the Head of the Ameera International School Center, with the aim of improving the welfare of teachers and employees of the Ameera International School Center at that time.

Ameera Coperation was first established in May 1969 and was named Koperasi Gotong Royong which at that time only managed the Business Unit & Savings and Loans. By mutual agreement between Teachers, Employees and Parents, in 1972 Ameera Coperation expanded its business by providing basic daily necessities to meet the needs of parents & students of Ameera International School in general as well as Teachers and Employees of Ameera Foundation in particular.
