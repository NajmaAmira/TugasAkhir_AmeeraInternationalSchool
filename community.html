PROFILE
HAJJ AMEERA FRATERNITY


The purpose of establishing the Ameera Hajj Brotherhood itself is as follows:

  • Strengthening Ukhuwah Islamiyah among fellow members of the Hajj Brotherhood

  • Assisting YPI Ameera in spreading Shi'ar Islam

  • Fostering and improving the quality of the Hajj Pilgrims and the candidates of the Ameera Hajj Pilgrims

  • Developing other worship charities

The scope of its activities includes:

  • Social

  • Funds and Ventures

  • Education

  • Information and Communication

Description of the activities of the Ameera Hajj Brotherhood:

  • Organizing Dhuha Ahad lectures

  • Organizing the deepening of Arabic and Qur'an interpretation per word. In collaboration with one of the UAI Lecturers, Mr. Nur Hezbollah, M. Hum as a resource person.

  • Hold turbans every second Sunday to finance orphans' schooling, natural disaster donations and others.

  • Participated in the implementation of the Yatim / Dhu'afa Mass Circumcision organized by the Ameera General and Dental Medicine Center (BPUG).

  • Participated in every Yatim and Dhu'afa compensation event held by the Takmir of the Great Mosque of Ameera.
